3.2Howthisdiffersfrom"justanothermodel"
A monolithic model-including an in-model Mixture-of-Experts-is one trained artifact:its experts live
inside oneparameterspace,are trained jointly,route through a gate fixed at training time,andmust
beresident together.MOK inverts every one of those properties,and each inversion is an
architecturalmechanism,notamodelproperty:
